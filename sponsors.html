Sponsor FAQ

Who will be at the event?

400+ builders, engineers, designers, students, founders, and business owners from across Utah. Friday skews toward learners and first-timers; Saturday brings the most technical builders for the hackathon.

Can I recruit at the event?

Yes. Every tier includes recruiting access. Bring flyers, set up at your booth, and talk to whoever you want. Bounty and Headline sponsors also get first right of refusal on hiring the winning team of their bounty.

What is a "bounty"?

A bounty is a real business problem your company brings to the hackathon. You provide the problem statement and, optionally, sample data. Teams compete to build the best solution on Saturday. You judge and pick winners.

When is the deadline to sponsor?

The earlier the better — tiers with stage time and named tracks fill up fast. We finalize sponsor placements 4 weeks before the event.

Can I combine a sponsorship with a custom package?

Absolutely. Venue sponsorship, food & beverage, university scholarships, and custom activations are all available as add-ons on any tier.

What does a typical ROI look like?

Past sponsors have made 2–5 hires directly from the event, generated hundreds of new signups for their products, and placed their brand in front of the most active builder community in the state.
