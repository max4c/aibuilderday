04 · Rules

How it works.

General rules

These apply to every team. Each track has its own additional rules, pitched live at 1:05 PM Friday during Bounty Presentations.

01Build during the event.

Fresh code, fresh ideas. You can bring boilerplate and dependencies, but the core build happens on-site between Friday afternoon and Saturday 2 PM.

02Use any tools you want.

Any model, framework, library, API. Bring your own laptop and your own accounts. We'll have credit sponsors covering most of what you'll reach for.

03Compete for as many tracks as you want.

One project can be submitted to multiple tracks. Just check each track's rules before you commit. They vary.

04Demo to win.

Code freezes Saturday at 2 PM. You demo to judges and sponsors live. Awards at 4 PM.

05Be excellent to each other.

Help your neighbors. Mentors, sponsors, and judges are all here to help. Go talk to them.
