What is AI Builder Day?

Two days to spark new companies, ship tools sponsors will actually use, and connect technical and non-technical builders. Where future founders meet co-founders, problems get solved, and people find jobs.

Presented by the Startup State Initiative & JustBuild. Hosted at JobNimbus HQ.

Who is JustBuild?

JustBuild is Utah's premier AI community on a mission to make Utah AI-native. With a thriving community of 400+ engineers, founders, and builders, we host hackathons, coworking sessions, and events that bring together the best talent in the state.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Anyone. No credentials required.

Is it free?

Yes.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Friday is designed for complete beginners.

Do I need a team?

No. Teams form Saturday morning.

Where is it?

JobNimbus HQ in Draper, UT.

What's the difference between Friday and Saturday?

Friday = education and speakers. Saturday = building and competing.
